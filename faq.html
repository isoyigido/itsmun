How do I register to participate in Model UNs? +
You can register by going to the 'Applications' tab on this website.
What is Model United Nations (MUN)? +
Model United Nations (MUN) is an educational simulation where students represent different countries and engage in debates, negotiations, and decision-making to solve global issues. Participants act as delegates of their assigned countries in a variety of UN committees, following diplomatic rules and procedures to draft resolutions addressing specific topics.
What are the main skills developed in MUN? +
  • Public speaking
  • Negotiation and diplomacy
  • Research and analysis
  • Problem-solving
  • Critical thinking
  • Collaboration and teamwork
  • Leadership
How are awards given in MUN? +
  • Best Delegate: Given to the most outstanding delegate in a committee.
  • Outstanding Delegate: Given to delegates who significantly contributed to debate.
  • Honorable Mention: Awarded to delegates who performed well.
How can I prepare for my first MUN conference? +
  1. Research: Study your assigned country, the topic, and your committee’s role.
  2. Write a Position Paper: Draft a paper outlining your country’s stance.
  3. Learn Rules of Procedure: Familiarize yourself with the specific rules of the conference.
  4. Practice Public Speaking: Rehearse opening speeches and responses to questions.
  5. Review Diplomacy Skills: Prepare to negotiate and build consensus.
